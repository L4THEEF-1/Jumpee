HISTORY

JUMPEE is a run and gun indie video game developed and published by DaTheefStudios™. First announced in 2025, the game was released for Microsoft Windows and Xbox One in September 11th, 2025, for macOS in July 7th, 2028, for Nintendo Switch in April 9th, 2029, and for PlayStation 4 in August 31th, 2029. The game was inspired by the pixel art used in various game of 1980s and seeks to emulate their subversive and surrealist qualities.
