A Platformer Focused on Defeating the Boss

JUMPEE is a classic run and gun action game heavily focused on boss battles. Inspired by the pixel art used in various cartoons of 1980s, the visuals and audio are painstakingly created with the pixel art drawing frame-by-frame to make the game feel more depth and smooth.

Genre: Platformer
Platform: Web & Desktop
Target Audience: Younger Kids To Teenagers
